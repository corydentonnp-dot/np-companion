First-Time Setup Instructions
  1. Click Launch Chrome above. A separate Chrome window opens with a dedicated debug profile.
  2. In that Chrome window, navigate to NetPractice and log in with your credentials.
  3. Your login cookies are saved in the debug profile directory — you will stay logged in across restarts.
  4. If the session expires, NP Companion will show a re-auth banner on the dashboard. Just log in again in the debug Chrome window.

Why a separate profile? Chrome 136+ requires a dedicated --user-data-dir for remote debugging (CDP). The debug profile keeps automation cookies isolated from your personal browsing.
