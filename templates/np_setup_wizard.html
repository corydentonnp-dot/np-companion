How the Scraper Navigation Works

After logging in, the scraper follows these steps to reach your schedule:

  1. Login — Fills in Client Number, Username, Password and clicks Log In (automatic)
  2. Your steps below — Click "Schedule", then "Review Patient Appointments", etc.
  3. Verify schedule page — Confirms your provider name appears on the page
  4. Scrape patients — Clicks each patient name, reads details, presses Alt+Back (automatic)

Steps 1, 3, and 4 are handled automatically. You only need to define step 2 — the clicks to get from the post-login home page to your daily schedule.
